Frequently Asked Questions About Air Ventilators

How do air ventilators work without electricity?

Our air ventilators utilize the natural force of wind and thermal convection to create rotation. Even a slight breeze causes the ventilator to spin, creating a low-pressure area that draws hot air upward and out of the building. This continuous air extraction requires no electricity and operates 24/7 as long as there is even minimal air movement.
