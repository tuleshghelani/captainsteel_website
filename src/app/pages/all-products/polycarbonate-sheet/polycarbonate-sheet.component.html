Premium Polycarbonate Sheets

Captain Steel's polycarbonate sheets combine exceptional transparency with unmatched durability for versatile architectural and construction applications.

Our premium polycarbonate sheets are manufactured using advanced engineering processes and high-grade resins to deliver outstanding optical clarity, impact resistance, and weather durability. These lightweight yet incredibly strong sheets are up to 200 times stronger than glass while weighing only half as much.

With excellent UV protection, thermal insulation properties, and fire resistance, our polycarbonate sheets are the ideal solution for skylights, canopies, greenhouse glazing, industrial rooflights, architectural features, and numerous other applications requiring transparent, durable material with superior performance characteristics.
