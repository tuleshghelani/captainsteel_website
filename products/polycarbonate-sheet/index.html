Premium Polycarbonate Sheets

Captain Steel's polycarbonate sheets combine exceptional transparency with unmatched durability for versatile architectural and construction applications.

Our premium polycarbonate sheets are manufactured using advanced engineering processes and high-grade resins to deliver outstanding optical clarity, impact resistance, and weather durability. These lightweight yet incredibly strong sheets are up to 200 times stronger than glass while weighing only half as much.

With excellent UV protection, thermal insulation properties, and fire resistance, our polycarbonate sheets are the ideal solution for skylights, canopies, greenhouse glazing, industrial rooflights, architectural features, and numerous other applications requiring transparent, durable material with superior performance characteristics.

Key Features

Our polycarbonate sheets are engineered with advanced features for optimal performance across diverse applications

Superior Impact Resistance

Virtually unbreakable sheets with 200 times the impact strength of glass, ideal for areas prone to hail or other impacts.

UV Protection

Co-extruded UV protective layer blocks harmful radiation while allowing light transmission, preventing yellowing and degradation.

Weather Resistance

Excellent durability in extreme temperatures from -40°C to 120°C, withstanding harsh weather conditions year-round.

Fire Resistance

Self-extinguishing material with high fire resistance ratings meeting international safety standards.

Optical Clarity

Exceptional light transmission up to 90% with options for diffused or tinted sheets for specific lighting requirements.

Lightweight

Half the weight of glass with superior strength, reducing structural support requirements and installation costs.

Applications

Discover the versatility of our polycarbonate sheets across diverse industries and applications

Residential Applications

Perfect for skylights, patio covers, carports, and greenhouse glazing in residential settings.

  • Natural daylight with UV protection
  • Exceptional durability against weather elements
  • Attractive, modern aesthetic appeal

Commercial Architecture

Ideal for shopping malls, office buildings, and exhibition centers requiring transparent roofing and glazing solutions.

  • Energy-efficient natural lighting
  • Excellent acoustic properties
  • Design flexibility with various colors and finishes

Industrial Facilities

Used in factories, warehouses, and processing plants as rooflights, partitions, and safety barriers.

  • Impact resistance for safety
  • High light transmission for reduced energy costs
  • Low maintenance requirements

Agricultural Structures

Perfect for greenhouses, nurseries, and livestock shelters providing controlled environments for growth.

  • Optimal light diffusion for plant growth
  • Excellent thermal insulation
  • Resistant to agricultural chemicals

Infrastructure Projects

  • Vandal resistance and durability
  • Weather protection with visibility
  • Long-term cost-effectiveness

Recreational Facilities
